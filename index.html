ABSIKO SWEDEN

The northern lights shimmer right across the polar rtegions – if solar activity is particularly exuberant you might even glimpse the lights as far south as Scotland.

MARFA LIGHTS TEXAS

As illuminating experiences go, this one really isn’t – you can see the Marfa Lights flashing over the Chihuahuan Desert and still not have a clue what they are.

SVALBARD NORWAY

Wish there were more hours in the day? No problem, plan a summer trip to Svalbard. Nudging the North Pole, this Arctic archipelago has long, dark, gloomy winters but makes up for it come spring.

TOYAMA BAY JAPAN

It’s like the sea is having a really big party. Every year, from March to May, the waters of north-central Honshu’s Toyama Bay, Japan, start to sparkle like a glitterball.

NIGHT AMSTERDAM

No need to indulge in the famed cafe narcotics to have your mind blown in Amsterdam – Electric Ladyland will do the job.

FESTIVAL OF LIGHTS

Berlin never looks more brilliant than during its Festival of Lights. For 10 days each October, the landmarks and thoroughfares of the German capital become 3D canvases for a glittering array of art.
